<p><strong>The PoE Chipset Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Integrated Chipset</li><li>Non-Integrated Chipset</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the PoE Chipset market, there are two main types: Integrated Chipset and Non-Integrated Chipset. Integrated chipsets combine both power sourcing equipment (PSE) and powered device (PD) functions into a single chip, offering a compact and efficient solution. On the other hand, non-integrated chipsets separate the PSE and PD functions into different chips, providing more flexibility and customization options for manufacturers. Both types cater to different market needs and offer various advantages depending on the application requirements.</p></p>
