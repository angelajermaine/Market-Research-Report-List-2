<p><p>Automotive Parking Sensor market features two main types, Forward and Rear View sensors. Forward parking sensors are designed to help drivers safely navigate tight spaces by alerting them to obstacles in front of the vehicle. Rear View sensors serve a similar purpose, but for the back of the vehicle. Both types utilize proximity sensors to detect objects and emit warning signals to the driver, preventing potential collisions and enhancing overall parking safety. These sensors have become increasingly popular in modern vehicles for added convenience and security.</p></p>
