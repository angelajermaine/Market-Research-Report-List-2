<p><p>Aluminum Alloy Automotive Wheels come in two main types: Forging Aluminum Alloy Wheels and Casting Aluminum Alloy Wheels. Forged wheels are made by applying extreme pressure to shape the aluminum, resulting in a stronger and lighter wheel. Cast wheels, on the other hand, are made by pouring molten aluminum into a mold, making them more affordable but slightly heavier. Both types offer benefits depending on the specific needs of the vehicle and driver, ranging from performance to budget considerations.</p></p>
