<p><p>Film antislip additives are chemicals added to films or coatings to provide traction and prevent slips or falls. The two main types of film antislip additives are aluminum silicate and silica. Aluminum silicate additives are derived from clay minerals and are known for their high temperature resistance and durability. Silica additives are made from sand or quartz particles and are valued for their low cost and effectiveness in creating a slip-resistant surface. Both types of additives play a crucial role in enhancing the safety and usability of various film applications.</p></p>
