<p><p>The aviation cargo system bearing market consists of two main types: standard bearings and medium pressure bearings. Standard bearings are designed for general cargo transportation requirements, offering durability and reliability under normal operating conditions. Medium pressure bearings are specifically engineered to withstand increased stress and pressure encountered during the handling of heavy or oversized cargo. These bearings are built to ensure smooth and efficient operation of the cargo system, even under challenging conditions. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the safe and efficient transport of goods in the aviation industry.</p></p>
