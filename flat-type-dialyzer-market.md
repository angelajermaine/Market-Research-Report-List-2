<p><strong>The Flat Type Dialyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Flux Membrane</li><li>High Flux Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flat type dialyzer market is segmented into two types: Low Flux Membrane and High Flux Membrane. Low Flux Membrane dialyzers are designed for patients with mild to moderate kidney failure, removing waste products and excess fluid from the blood at a slower rate. In contrast, High Flux Membrane dialyzers are used for patients with severe kidney failure, offering better clearance of waste products and toxins due to their larger pore size. Both types play a crucial role in providing effective hemodialysis treatment for patients with kidney failure.</p></p>
