<p><p>Radial tyres come in three main types based on their construction: Whole Steel Wire, Half-Steel Wire, and Whole Fiber. Whole Steel Wire tyres have a full steel belt construction for increased strength and durability. Half-Steel Wire tyres combine steel belts with other materials for a balance of performance and cost-effectiveness. Whole Fiber tyres use fiber belts instead of steel for lighter weight and improved fuel efficiency. These different types cater to varying needs regarding performance, longevity, and cost in the radial tyre market.</p></p>
