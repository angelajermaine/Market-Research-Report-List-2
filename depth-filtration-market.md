<p><p>Depth filtration is a type of filtration process where particles are captured within a porous filtration medium such as filter paper, filter cloth, or depth filter cartridges. This method relies on the size of the particles and their ability to penetrate the depth of the filter medium, rather than surface filtration where particles are captured on the surface of the filter medium.</p><p>The depth filtration market is expected to experience significant growth in the coming years, with a projected CAGR of 11.5% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to the increasing demand for depth filtration in various industries such as pharmaceutical, biotechnology, food and beverage, and water treatment. The versatility and efficiency of depth filtration make it a preferred choice for many applications where high-quality filtration is required.</p><p>Some of the latest trends in the depth filtration market include the development of advanced depth filter media with enhanced filtration capabilities, the increasing adoption of automated filtration systems for improved efficiency, and the growing prominence of disposable depth filter cartridges for easy handling and reduced contamination risks.
