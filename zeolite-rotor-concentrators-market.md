<p><p>Zeolite Rotor Concentrators are air pollution control devices that use zeolite as an adsorbent material to capture volatile organic compounds (VOCs) from industrial air streams. These concentrators work by rotating a honeycomb rotor that is coated with zeolite, allowing the VOCs to be adsorbed onto the zeolite surface, which can then be regenerated.</p><p>The Zeolite Rotor Concentrators Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.4% during the forecast period.
