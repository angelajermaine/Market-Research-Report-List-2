<p><p>The automotive ignition switch market consists of two types: key ignition and button ignition systems. Key ignition systems require the driver to insert a physical key into the ignition switch and turn it to start the vehicle. On the other hand, button ignition systems allow the driver to start the vehicle with the push of a button, without the need for a physical key. Both types of ignition systems serve the same function of starting the vehicle's engine, but offer different mechanisms for doing so.</p></p>
