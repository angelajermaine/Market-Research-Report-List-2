<p><p>Dispersants market types include Anionic Type, Cationic Type, and Others. Anionic dispersants have a negative charge and are commonly used to disperse solids in water-based systems. Cationic dispersants have a positive charge and are effective in dispersing materials like clays and pigments. Other types of dispersants include non-ionic dispersants, which do not carry a charge but are still effective in dispersing materials. Each type of dispersant has specific characteristics that make them suitable for different applications in industries such as paints, coatings, and agriculture.</p></p>
