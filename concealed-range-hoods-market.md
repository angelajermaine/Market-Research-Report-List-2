<p><strong>The Concealed Range Hoods Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Axial Flow</li><li>Centrifugal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Concealed range hoods are available in two main market types: axial flow and centrifugal. Axial flow range hoods use a fan blade to pull air out in a straight line, making them more suitable for smaller kitchens. Centrifugal range hoods, on the other hand, use a fan that spins air in a circular motion, making them more powerful and better suited for larger kitchens with heavy cooking. Both types of range hoods are popular choices for homeowners looking to keep their kitchens well-ventilated and free of cooking odors.</p></p>
