<p><p>Light vehicle transmissions can be broadly categorized into five types: Manual Transmission (MT), Automatic Transmission (AT), Automated Manual Transmission (AMT), Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT), and Dual Clutch Transmission (DCT). MT requires manual shifting of gears by the driver, while AT shifts gears automatically. AMT is a blend of manual and automatic transmission. CVT provides seamless shifting through a continuous range of gear ratios, and DCT offers fast and smooth gear changes through dual clutches. These different transmission types cater to varying driving preferences and fuel efficiency needs in the automotive market.</p></p>
