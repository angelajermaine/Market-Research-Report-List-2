<p><ul><li>Bushings</li><li>Journal Bearings</li><li>Sleeve Bearings</li><li>Riffle Bearing</li><li>Composite Bearing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Plain bearings motors utilize various types of bearings to support rotating shafts and reduce friction. These include bushings, journal bearings, sleeve bearings, riffle bearings, and composite bearings. Bushings are cylindrical metal sleeves that reduce friction and support radial loads. Journal bearings support heavy radial loads and are commonly used in high-speed applications. Sleeve bearings are simple and cost-effective options for low-speed applications. Riffle bearings have a unique design for improved lubrication. Composite bearings combine different materials for enhanced performance in specific applications.</p></p>
