<p><strong>The Hepatitis A Vaccine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inactivated Vaccine</li><li>Live Attenuated Vaccine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hepatitis A vaccines are available in two main types: inactivated vaccine and live attenuated vaccine. Inactivated vaccines contain a killed version of the hepatitis A virus, which is unable to cause disease but can still trigger an immune response. Live attenuated vaccines contain a weakened, but still live, version of the virus. Both types aim to stimulate the immune system to produce antibodies against the hepatitis A virus, providing immunity against future infection. The choice of vaccine type depends on factors like age, health status, and potential contraindications.</p></p>
