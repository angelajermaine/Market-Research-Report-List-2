<p><p>Amino-modified silicone emulsion is categorized into different types based on viscosity levels: Low Viscosity, Medium & High Viscosity, and Ultra-high Viscosity. Low Viscosity emulsions have a thinner consistency and are suitable for applications where a lighter coating is desired. Medium & High Viscosity emulsions offer a balance of thickness and flow, making them versatile for various applications. Ultra-high Viscosity emulsions have a thicker consistency, providing greater film build and enhanced durability, ideal for demanding coating requirements.</p></p>
