<p><ul><li>Mechanical Liner Hanger Systems</li><li>Hydraulic Liner Hanger Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Liner hanger systems are essential tools used in the oil and gas industry to hang and support casing liners in wells. There are two main types of liner hanger systems: Mechanical Liner Hanger Systems and Hydraulic Liner Hanger Systems. Mechanical systems are set using mechanical slips and are typically simpler and more cost-effective. Hydraulic systems, on the other hand, use hydraulic pressure to set the hanger in place and are known for their reliability and ability to withstand high pressure and temperature environments.</p></p>
