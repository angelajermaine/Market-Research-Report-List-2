<p><p>Refractive Optical Element (ROE) market types include Beam Shaping / Top-Hat, Beam Splitting, and Beam Foci. Beam Shaping / Top-Hat ROEs are used to achieve uniform intensity distribution in laser applications. Beam Splitting ROEs divide an input beam into multiple output beams for various applications. Beam Foci ROEs are designed to focus a beam to a desired spot size for precise targeting. Each type of ROE serves a specific purpose in manipulating the characteristics of light beams for optimal performance in diverse industrial and scientific applications.</p></p>
