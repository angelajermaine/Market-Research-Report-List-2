<p><p>Photovoltaic roof tiles market consists of two main types: Monocrystalline and Polycrystalline. Monocrystalline tiles are made from single-crystal silicon, making them more efficient and space-saving but also more expensive. Polycrystalline tiles, on the other hand, are made from multiple silicon fragments, making them more affordable but slightly less efficient. Both types of photovoltaic roof tiles offer sustainable energy solutions for residential and commercial buildings, contributing to the growing popularity of renewable energy sources.</p></p>
