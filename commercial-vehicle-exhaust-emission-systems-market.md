<p><p>Commercial Vehicle Exhaust Emission Systems market includes Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR) Systems and Exhaust Gas Recirculation (EGR) Systems. SCR Systems use a catalyst to convert harmful nitrogen oxide emissions into nitrogen and water vapor. EGR Systems work by recirculating a portion of exhaust gases back into the engine to reduce the combustion temperature and limit nitrogen oxide production. Both systems are essential in meeting strict emission regulations and improving air quality in commercial vehicles.</p></p>
