<p><strong>The Optical Fiber Gyroscope Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Interferometric</li><li>Resonant</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical Fiber Gyroscope market is segmented into three main types - Interferometric, Resonant, and Others. Interferometric gyros are based on the Sagnac effect and measure the phase shift between counter-propagating light beams. Resonant gyros utilize the resonant frequency of a vibrating structure to measure rotation. Other types in the market include micro-electromechanical systems (MEMS) gyros and ring laser gyros. Each type offers unique advantages and is used in various applications such as navigation systems, aircraft, and industrial equipment.</p></p>
