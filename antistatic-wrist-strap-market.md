<p><strong>The Antistatic Wrist Strap Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Corded Wrist Strap</li><li>Cordless Wrist Strap</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Antistatic wrist straps are used in electronic workplaces to prevent static electricity from damaging sensitive equipment. There are two main types of antistatic wrist straps on the market: corded wrist straps and cordless wrist straps.</p><p>Corded wrist straps have a grounding cord that connects the strap to an electrical outlet or grounding point, providing a continuous path for static electricity to flow safely away from the user. Cordless wrist straps, on the other hand, use a built-in battery or capacitor to create a static-free environment without the need for a cord.</p></p>
