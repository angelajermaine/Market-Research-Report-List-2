<p><p>An HDMI Extender is a device used to extend the reach of an HDMI signal from a source device to a display, over long distances. This technology is commonly used in settings where the source device and display are located far apart, such as in conference rooms, classrooms, or home theater setups. HDMI Extenders typically consist of a transmitter unit that connects to the source device and a receiver unit that connects to the display, with a Cat5e/6 cable running between them to transmit the signal.</p><p>The HDMI Extender Market is witnessing significant growth, driven by the increasing demand for high-quality audio and video transmission over long distances.
