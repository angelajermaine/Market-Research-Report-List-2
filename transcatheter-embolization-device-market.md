<p><strong>The Transcatheter Embolization Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Particle embolic</li><li>Coil embolic</li><li>Liquid embolic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Transcatheter embolization devices are used to block blood vessels or abnormal blood flow in various medical procedures. The three main types of devices in the market are particle embolic, coil embolic, and liquid embolic. Particle embolic devices use tiny particles to block blood flow, while coil embolic devices use coils to occlude vessels. Liquid embolic devices involve injecting a liquid substance that hardens inside the vessel to block blood flow. Each type of device has its own benefits and applications in medical procedures.</p></p>
