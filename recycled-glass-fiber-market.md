<p><ul><li>Mechanical Method</li><li>Calcination Method</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The recycled glass fiber market can be categorized into three types: Mechanical Method, Calcination Method, and Others. The Mechanical Method involves crushing and grinding glass waste to produce fibers. The Calcination Method involves heating glass waste to high temperatures to create fibers. The Others category includes various methods such as plasma spraying and sol-gel processes. Each of these methods plays a crucial role in recycling glass waste and producing sustainable glass fiber products for various industries.</p></p>
