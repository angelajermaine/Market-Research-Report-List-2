<p><strong>The Mobile Sandblasting Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gravity feed Sandblasting Machine</li><li>Pressure Sandblasting Machine</li><li>Siphon Sandblasting Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mobile sandblasting machines come in three main types: Gravity feed, Pressure, and Siphon. Gravity feed machines use gravity to feed abrasive material into the blasting gun, Pressure machines use compressed air to force abrasive material through the nozzle at high velocity, and Siphon machines mix air and abrasive material in a chamber before propelling it out. Each type has its own advantages and is suitable for different applications, depending on factors like the material being blasted and the desired outcome.</p></p>
