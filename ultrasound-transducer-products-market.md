<p><p>Ultrasound transducer products are categorized into different types based on their design and functionality. The main market types include linear, convex, phased array, endocavitary, continuous wave (CW) Doppler, and others. </p><p>Linear transducers are used for high-resolution imaging in superficial areas, convex transducers are ideal for abdominal and obstetric imaging, phased array transducers are used for cardiac and vascular imaging, endocavitary transducers are designed for endocavity applications, CW Doppler transducers are used for measuring blood flow, and other types cater to specific imaging needs or specialties.</p></p>
