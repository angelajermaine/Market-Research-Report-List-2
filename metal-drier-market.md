<p><p>Metal driers are a type of additive used in oil-based coatings, inks, and other products to accelerate the drying process by promoting oxidation and polymerization reactions. They typically contain metal compounds such as cobalt, manganese, and zirconium, which act as catalysts to speed up the drying time of the product. The metal drier market is witnessing significant growth due to the increasing demand for fast-drying coatings and inks in various industries such as automotive, construction, and packaging.</p><p>The Metal Drier Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.3% during the forecast period. Additionally, advancements in technology and the development of new formulations are also contributing to the growth of the metal drier market.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the metal drier market is the shift towards water-based coatings and inks, which are more environmentally friendly and less hazardous than solvent-based products. Additionally, there is a growing emphasis on innovation and customization to cater to the specific requirements of different end-users in various industries.</p></p>
