<p><p>Super Clear Glass is a type of glass known for its high level of clarity and transparency. In the market, there are two main types of Super Clear Glass: Rolled Glass and Float Glass. Rolled Glass is made by pouring molten glass onto a metal table and rolling it into a sheet, while Float Glass is made by floating molten glass on top of molten tin to create a smooth, uniform surface. Both types are widely used in architectural and decorative applications due to their superior clarity and high level of transparency.</p></p>
