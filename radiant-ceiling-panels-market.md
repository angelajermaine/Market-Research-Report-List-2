<p><p>Radiant ceiling panels are a type of heating system that can be powered using either water heating or electric heating methods. Water heating systems use heated water to radiate heat through the ceiling panels, while electric heating systems use electricity to generate heat. Both types of radiant ceiling panels are popular in residential, commercial, and industrial settings for their energy efficiency and uniform heating distribution. Water heating systems are generally considered more cost-effective in the long run, while electric heating systems offer quicker and more customizable heating options.</p></p>
