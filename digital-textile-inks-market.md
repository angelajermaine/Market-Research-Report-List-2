<p><p>Digital Textile Inks come in various types, each catering to different fabric printing needs. Dispersed Inks are commonly used for polyester fabrics as they are compatible with high-speed printing. Reactive Inks are ideal for natural fibers like cotton, as they form a strong bond with the fabric. Acid Inks are suitable for protein fibers such as silk and wool, providing vibrant colors. Pigment Inks are versatile and can be used on a wide range of fabrics, offering excellent color fastness. Each type of ink plays a crucial role in the digital textile printing market.</p></p>
