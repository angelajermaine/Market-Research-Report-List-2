<p><p>Advanced Therapy Medicinal Products (ATMPs) are innovative therapies that harness the power of cells, genes, or tissues to treat diseases. Cell therapy uses living cells to repair damaged tissue and improve immune response. CAR-T therapy involves modifying a patient's own immune cells to target and destroy cancer cells. Gene therapy replaces faulty genes with healthy ones to treat genetic disorders. Tissue-engineered products are artificial tissues created in a lab and used to repair or replace damaged organs. Each type of ATMP offers unique potential for personalized and effective treatments.</p></p>
