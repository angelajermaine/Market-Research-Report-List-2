<p><p>Bioabsorbable polymers (BP) are materials that can be broken down and absorbed by the body over time. In the market, there are two main types of BP: single polymer and copolymer. </p><p>Single polymer BPs are made from a single type of polymer, which provides uniform degradation and absorption rates. Copolymer BPs are made from a combination of different polymers, offering customizable properties such as strength, flexibility, and degradation rates. Both single polymer and copolymer BPs are widely used in various medical applications, such as sutures, drug delivery systems, and tissue engineering.</p></p>
