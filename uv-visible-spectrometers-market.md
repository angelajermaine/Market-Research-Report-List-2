<p><p>UV-Visible Spectrometers are analytical instruments used to measure the absorption or transmission of ultraviolet and visible light by a sample. The market for UV-Vis spectrometers can be classified into two main types: Single-Beam UV-Vis Spectrophotometers and Double-Beam UV-Vis Spectrophotometers. Single-Beam spectrophotometers measure the sample and reference beams separately, while Double-Beam spectrophotometers measure both simultaneously, providing more accurate and reliable results. Both types of spectrophotometers are widely used in various industries such as pharmaceuticals, environmental testing, and research.</p></p>
