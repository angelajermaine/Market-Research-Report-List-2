<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Dual Band Wireless Router Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>802.11a</li><li>802.11b</li><li>802.11g</li><li>802.11n</li><li>802.11ac</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dual Band Wireless Routers cater to different market types based on the 802.11 standards they support. 802.11a operates on the 5GHz frequency band, providing fast speeds but limited range. 802.11b and 802.11g operate on the 2.4GHz frequency band, with g offering faster speeds than b. 802.11n operates on both 2.4GHz and 5GHz bands, providing improved range and speed. 802.11ac is the latest standard, operating on the 5GHz band and offering even faster speeds and better performance.</p></p>
