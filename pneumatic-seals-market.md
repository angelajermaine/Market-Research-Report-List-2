<p><p>Pneumatic seals are used in various industries to prevent leakage of air or fluids in pneumatic systems. There are three main types of pneumatic seals on the market: Ring Sealing Rings, Circular Sealing Rings, and Rectangular Sealing Rings. Ring Sealing Rings are designed to provide a tight seal in a circular motion, while Circular Sealing Rings are used for rotary applications. Rectangular Sealing Rings are ideal for sealing flat surfaces. Each type of seal is chosen based on the specific requirements of the pneumatic system.</p></p>
