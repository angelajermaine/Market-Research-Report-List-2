<p><ul><li>Large Granule Urea</li><li>Small Granule Urea</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Large Granule Urea and Small Granule Urea are two main types of urea used in agriculture. Large Granule Urea consists of urea particles that are larger in size and are mainly used in broadcasting applications for crops like corn and wheat. On the other hand, Small Granule Urea is made up of smaller urea particles that are suitable for precision application methods like side-dressing or fertigation in fruits and vegetable crops. Both types of urea serve as important sources of nitrogen fertilizer for improving crop yields and quality in agriculture.</p></p>
