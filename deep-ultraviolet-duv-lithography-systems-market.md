<p><ul><li>ArF Immersion</li><li>ArF Dry</li><li>KrF</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Deep Ultraviolet (DUV) lithography systems are primarily categorized into three types: ArF Immersion, ArF Dry, and KrF. ArF Immersion systems use a liquid medium to enhance resolution, while ArF Dry systems operate in a dry environment, offering cost-saving benefits. KrF systems utilize krypton fluoride as the light source for lithography. These systems are essential in the semiconductor industry for producing high-resolution microchips and other electronic components, driving advancements in technology and innovation.</p></p>
