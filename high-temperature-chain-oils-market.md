<p><p>High temperature chain oils are typically divided into two main market types: Synthetic oil and semi-synthetic oil. Synthetic oils are made entirely from chemically engineered compounds and offer superior thermal stability and resistance to oxidation compared to traditional mineral oils. Semi-synthetic oils, on the other hand, are a blend of synthetic and mineral oils, offering a balance between performance and cost-effectiveness. Both types of oils are used in high temperature applications where conventional oils would break down.</p></p>
