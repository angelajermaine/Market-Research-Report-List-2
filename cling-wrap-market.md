<p><p>Cling wrap is available in various market types, including PE (polyethylene), PVC (polyvinyl chloride), PVDC (polyvinylidene chloride), DEHA (diethylhexyl adipate), DOA (dioctyl adipate), and others. PE is a common choice due to its flexibility and cost-effectiveness. PVC offers good clarity and strength. PVDC provides excellent barrier properties. DEHA and DOA are plasticizers used to improve flexibility. Other market types may include biodegradable options or specialty materials for specific applications. Each type offers unique advantages and is used for different purposes in the cling wrap market.</p></p>
