<p><ul><li>Dumbbell Type</li><li>Center Bulb Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rubber waterstop market includes two main types: dumbbell type and center bulb type. The dumbbell type features two rounded bulbs on either side of a flat center section, offering effective sealing for construction joints. On the other hand, the center bulb type has a larger rounded bulb in the middle, providing increased flexibility and better protection against water leakage. Both types are commonly used in concrete structures to prevent water infiltration and ensure durability.</p></p>
