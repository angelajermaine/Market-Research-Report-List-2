<p><p>Phase Transfer Catalyst is a type of catalyst used in chemical reactions to facilitate the transfer of reactants between two immiscible phases, typically aqueous and organic. Two major types of Phase Transfer Catalysts are Ammonium Salts and Phosphonium Salts. Ammonium Salts are quaternary ammonium compounds with a positively charged nitrogen atom, while Phosphonium Salts are quaternary phosphonium compounds with a positively charged phosphorus atom. Both types of catalysts help improve the efficiency and selectivity of reactions that occur at the interface of different phases.</p></p>
