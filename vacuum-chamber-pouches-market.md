<p><p>Vacuum chamber pouches come in various types such as Polyethylene Terephthalate (PET), Polyethylene (PE), Polyamide (PA), Cast Polypropylene (CPP), and others. PET pouches are transparent, strong, and resistant to impact, making them ideal for food packaging. PE pouches are flexible and heat-sealable, commonly used for medical and industrial packaging. PA pouches provide excellent barrier properties against oxygen and water vapor. CPP pouches are durable and heat-resistant, suitable for frozen food packaging. Other types include materials like metalized films or foil laminates for specific packaging needs.</p></p>
