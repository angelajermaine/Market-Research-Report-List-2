<p><p>Overhead Ground Wire, also known as OPGW, is a type of electrical cable that is used in overhead power lines for grounding and lightning protection. It consists of a central core made of optical fibers surrounded by aluminum-clad steel wires for strength and conductivity. OPGW serves the dual purpose of carrying electrical signals for communication and providing a path for lightning strikes to safely reach the ground.</p><p>The global Overhead Ground Wire Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11% during the forecast period. The market growth is driven by the increasing demand for reliable and efficient transmission and distribution of electricity in both urban and rural areas. The growing investments in renewable energy projects and smart grid infrastructure are also contributing to the expansion of the OPGW market.</p><p>One of the key trends in the Overhead Ground Wire Market is the development of advanced design and materials to enhance the performance and durability of OPGW cables. Manufacturers are focusing on innovations such as higher fiber counts, improved shielding, and better corrosion resistance to meet the evolving needs of the power industry. Additionally, the adoption of OPGW for new transmission projects and retrofitting existing power lines with OPGW for improved reliability and safety is driving the market growth.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Central Tube Structure</li><li>Layer Stranding Structure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Overhead Ground Wire market includes two main types: Central Tube Structure and Layer Stranding Structure. Central Tube Structure consists of a central metallic tube surrounded by layers of aluminum wires, providing strength and durability. Layer Stranding Structure, on the other hand, involves multiple layers of aluminum wires twisted around a central core wire. Both types offer reliable grounding and protection against lightning strikes for overhead power lines. Customers can choose between these options based on their specific requirements and preferences.</p></p>
