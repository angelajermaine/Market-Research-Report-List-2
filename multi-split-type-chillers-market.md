<p><p>Multi-split type chillers are divided into two main types- air-cooled and water-cooled. Air-cooled chillers use ambient air to cool the refrigerant, making them suitable for outdoor installation and requiring less maintenance. On the other hand, water-cooled chillers use water to remove heat, offering greater efficiency and capacity but requiring a water source for operation. Both types cater to different requirements and environments, providing flexibility and options for various cooling needs in different settings.</p></p>
