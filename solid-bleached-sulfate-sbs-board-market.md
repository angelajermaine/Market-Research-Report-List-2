<p><p>Solid Bleached Sulfate (SBS) board can be categorized into two main types - SBS C1S (Coated One Side) and SBS C2S (Coated Two Side). SBS C1S board has a coating applied to only one side, making it ideal for applications where the focus is on the printed side. On the other hand, SBS C2S board has a coating applied to both sides, making it suitable for high-end printing and packaging applications where both sides need to be visually appealing.</p></p>
