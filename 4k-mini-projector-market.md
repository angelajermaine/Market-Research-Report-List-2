<p><p>The 4K Mini Projector market is divided into three main types based on the technology used: LCD (Liquid Crystal Display), DLP (Digital Light Processing), and LCOS (Liquid Crystal on Silicon). LCD projectors use liquid crystals to produce images, DLP projectors use mirrors to reflect light, and LCOS projectors combine liquid crystal and silicon technology. Each type of projector offers different benefits in terms of image quality, brightness, and color accuracy, catering to different consumer preferences and needs in the market.</p></p>
