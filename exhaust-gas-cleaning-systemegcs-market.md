<p><ul><li>Open Loop Exhaust Gas Purification System</li><li>Closed Loop Exhaust Gas Purification System</li><li>Mixed Waste Gas Purification System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Exhaust Gas Cleaning Systems (EGCS) refer to technologies that are utilized to reduce pollutants emitted from ship exhaust gases. The three main types of EGCS markets include Open Loop, Closed Loop, and Mixed Waste Gas Purification Systems. Open Loop systems use seawater to clean exhaust gases before they are discharged into the ocean, while Closed Loop systems recycle the wash water and treat it before discharge. Mixed Waste Gas Purification Systems combine both open and closed loop technologies to meet emission regulations.</p></p>
