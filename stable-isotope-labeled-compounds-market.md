<p><p>Stable isotope labeled compounds market consists of various types such as C13, D, O18, and N15 labeling. C13 labeling involves replacing a carbon atom in a molecule with a heavier isotope of carbon, which can aid in metabolic studies. D labeling involves replacing hydrogen atoms with deuterium, useful for drug development research. O18 and N15 labeling involve replacing oxygen and nitrogen atoms with heavier isotopes for tracking in biological studies. Each type has unique applications in different research fields.</p></p>
