<p><strong>The Reference Electrode Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Aqueous Reference Electrode</li><li>Nonaqueous Reference Electrode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Reference electrodes are essential components in various electrochemical processes. Two main types include aqueous and nonaqueous reference electrodes. </p><p>Aqueous reference electrodes are designed for use in aqueous solutions and are typically filled with a saturated solution of a specific salt. They provide a stable reference potential for measurements in water-based environments.</p><p>Nonaqueous reference electrodes, on the other hand, are used in non-aqueous environments such as organic solvents. They are designed to maintain a stable reference potential in these different chemical environments.</p></p>
