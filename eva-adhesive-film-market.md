<p><p>EVA Adhesive Film comes in two main types: Transparent EVA Film and White EVA Film. Transparent EVA Film is commonly used for applications where clarity is necessary, such as for laminating glass or screens. On the other hand, White EVA Film is often preferred for applications where opacity is required, such as in the production of whiteboards or signage. Both types offer excellent adhesion properties and are widely used in industries such as construction, packaging, and automotive.</p></p>
