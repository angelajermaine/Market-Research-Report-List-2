<p><ul><li>Single-spark</li><li>Multi-spark</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Single-spark ignition coils produce one spark per ignition cycle, suitable for most traditional engines. On the other hand, multi-spark ignition coils generate multiple sparks per cycle, maximizing fuel combustion efficiency and power output. Multi-spark coils are commonly used in high-performance engines where increased precision and power delivery are required. Both types play key roles in determining the performance and efficiency of light vehicle engines, catering to different needs and preferences in the automotive market.</p></p>
