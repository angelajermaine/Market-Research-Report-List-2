<p><p>Continuously Variable Transmissions (CVT) systems come in two main types - Metal Belt and Metal Chain. Metal Belt CVT systems use a metal belt to smoothly transfer power from the engine to the wheels, providing an efficient and seamless driving experience. On the other hand, Metal Chain CVT systems utilize a metal chain to achieve the same purpose. Both types offer advantages in terms of fuel efficiency, smooth acceleration, and overall performance, making them popular choices in the automotive industry.</p></p>
