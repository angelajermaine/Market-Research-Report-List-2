<p><strong>The Chemically Modified Wood Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Acetylated Wood</li><li>Furfurylized wood</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Chemically modified wood markets like acetylated wood and furfurylized wood are gaining popularity due to their enhanced properties. Acetylated wood undergoes a process where acetic anhydride is used to modify the wood structure, resulting in increased durability and resistance to decay. On the other hand, furfurylized wood is treated with furfuryl alcohol, creating a polymer within the wood fibers that improves its strength and resistance to water. Both types of chemically modified wood are increasingly being used in various applications, including outdoor decking and furniture.</p></p>
