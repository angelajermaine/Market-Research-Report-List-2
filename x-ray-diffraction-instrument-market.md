<p><p>X-Ray Diffraction Instrument Market can be divided into two main types: Powder XRD and Single-crystal XRD. </p><p>Powder XRD is used for analyzing powdered samples and is widely used in pharmaceuticals, materials science, and geological studies. It provides information on the crystal structure, phase identification, and crystallinity of materials.</p><p>Single-crystal XRD, on the other hand, is specifically designed for analyzing single crystals and is commonly used in the field of crystallography. It provides detailed information on the atomic and molecular structure of crystals.</p></p>
