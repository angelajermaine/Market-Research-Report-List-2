<p><p>Automotive Fuel Tank Pressure Sensors are categorized into three types based on the pressure they can measure: Low Pressure Pressure Sensor, Medium Pressure Pressure Sensor, and High Pressure Pressure Sensor. Low Pressure sensors typically measure pressures below 30 psi, while Medium Pressure sensors measure pressures ranging from 30 to 100 psi. High Pressure sensors, on the other hand, are capable of measuring pressures above 100 psi. Each type of sensor is designed to accurately measure the specific pressure range required for fuel tank monitoring in vehicles.</p></p>
