<p><p>Brushless AC servo motors are categorized into three markets based on voltage requirements: low voltage, mid voltage, and high voltage. Low voltage motors typically operate at voltages below 1000V and are commonly used in smaller applications such as robotics and automation systems. Mid voltage motors operate at voltages between 1000V and 6000V, suitable for medium-sized industrial applications. High voltage motors operate at voltages above 6000V, designed for large-scale industrial applications such as machinery and equipment in sectors like oil and gas, power generation, and mining.</p></p>
