<p><p>The ceramic radiant panel market is divided into three main types based on their structure and design: tube-type, rod type, and other variations. Tube-type panels consist of a series of ceramic tubes that emit radiant heat when heated. Rod-type panels are composed of ceramic rods that also emit radiant heat. The "other" category includes innovative designs and structures that do not fall into the tube or rod types, offering different variations and options for consumers looking for ceramic radiant panels.</p></p>
