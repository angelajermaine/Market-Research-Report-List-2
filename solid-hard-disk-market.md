<p><p>A solid hard disk, also known as a Solid State Drive (SSD), is a data storage device that uses solid-state memory to store data. Manufacturers are also focusing on increasing the storage capacities of SSDs while reducing their costs to make them more accessible to a wider range of consumers. Additionally, advancements in 3D NAND technology are enabling SSDs to achieve higher capacities and better performance, further driving market growth.</p></p>
