<p><ul><li>Surface Capacitive Tactile Sensor</li><li>Projected Capacitive Tactile Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Capacitive Tactile Sensors are divided into two main types, namely Surface Capacitive Tactile Sensors and Projected Capacitive Tactile Sensors. Surface capacitive sensors are designed to detect touch from a finger or stylus directly on the surface of the sensor. Projected capacitive sensors, on the other hand, use multiple layers of transparent conductive material to detect touch by sensing changes in capacitance. Both types of sensors are widely used in touchscreens, touch panels, and other touch-sensitive devices in various industries.</p></p>
