<p><p>Fermentation-Derived Cellulose market can be categorized into three types based on its physical properties: Suspension, Dispersibility, and Dilutes. Suspension cellulose is typically used in products where the cellulose needs to be evenly distributed throughout the solution. Dispersibility cellulose refers to cellulose that has good dispersibility in various solvents and can be easily mixed. Dilutes cellulose is a type that can be easily diluted without losing its properties. Each type caters to different applications in industries such as food, pharmaceuticals, and cosmetics.</p></p>
