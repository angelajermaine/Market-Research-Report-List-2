<p><p>Mining pumps are classified into three types based on their capacity: Small (up to 500 gpm), Medium (500-1000 gpm), and High (more than 1000 gpm). Small pumps are typically used for smaller-scale operations or for specific tasks within larger projects. Medium pumps are suitable for a wide range of applications in medium-sized mining operations. High-capacity pumps are essential for large-scale mining projects that require a high volume of water movement. Each type of pump caters to specific needs within the mining industry.</p></p>

Jetting pumps are employed for high-pressure water jetting tasks. Water/wastewater pumps are essential for water treatment processes. Other applications of mining pumps include dewatering, process water supply, and tailings disposal.</p></p>
