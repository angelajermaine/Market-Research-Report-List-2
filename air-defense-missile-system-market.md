<p><p>Air-defense missile systems are classified into two main types: Man-Portable Air-Defense Systems (MANPADS) and Stationary/Platform-Mounted Air-Defense Systems. MANPADS are lightweight and portable, designed for individual soldiers to carry and operate on foot. Stationary/Platform-Mounted systems are larger, more powerful defense systems that are mounted on vehicles, ships, or fixed locations for protection against aircraft and missiles. Both types are essential components of air defense systems, providing a crucial defense against aerial threats.</p></p>
