<p><p>Synthetic rubber market types include Styrene Butadiene Rubber (SBR), Polybutadiene Rubber (BR), Styrenic Block Copolymer (SBC), Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer (EPDM), Butyl Rubber (IIR), Nitrile Butadiene Rubber (NBR), and others. SBR is widely used for tire manufacturing due to its high abrasion resistance. BR is used in the production of tires and industrial rubber goods. SBC is used in adhesives, sealants, and footwear. EPDM is used in automotive weather sealing. IIR is known for its impermeability to gases and is used in inner tubes and pharmaceutical stoppers. NBR is known for its oil and fuel resistance and is used in hoses and gaskets. Other synthetic rubber types cater to specific applications and industries.</p></p>
