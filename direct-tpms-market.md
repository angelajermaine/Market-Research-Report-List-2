<p><strong>The Direct TPMS Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High Line System</li><li>Low Line System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Direct TPMS market is divided into two types, High Line System and Low Line System. High Line System uses sensors within the tire to monitor pressure and temperature, providing accurate and real-time data to the driver. This system is typically found in luxury vehicles and offers advanced features and capabilities. On the other hand, Low Line System uses wheel speed sensors to indirectly monitor tire pressure, offering a more basic and cost-effective solution. This system is commonly found in budget-friendly vehicles.</p></p>
