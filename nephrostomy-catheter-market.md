<p><p>A nephrostomy catheter is a thin tube inserted through the skin into the kidney to drain urine in cases where the normal flow of urine is obstructed. This catheter helps in relieving pressure in the kidney and allows for the drainage of urine directly into a collection bag outside the body. Nephrostomy catheters are commonly used in patients with kidney stones, tumors, or other blockages that prevent urine from flowing freely.</p><p>The Nephrostomy Catheter Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.5% during the forecast period.
