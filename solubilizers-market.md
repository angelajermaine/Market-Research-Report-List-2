<p><p>Solubilizers are substances that help to enhance the solubility of poorly soluble substances in a solvent. Polysorbates are a type of solubilizer that consists of a mixture of sorbitol, ethylene oxide, and fatty acids, commonly known as Tween. Polyoxyethylene Fatty Acid Esters are another type of solubilizer that consist of a combination of fatty acids and polyethylene glycol. Both types of solubilizers are commonly used in the pharmaceutical, cosmetic, and food industries to improve the solubility and stability of various ingredients.</p></p>
