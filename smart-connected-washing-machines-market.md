<p><p>Smart connected washing machines come in two main types - top load and front load. Top load washing machines are more traditional and have a vertically-aligned drum, making them easier to load and unload. Front load washing machines, on the other hand, have a horizontally-aligned drum, which allows for gentler washing and less water usage. Both types of machines can be equipped with smart features such as Wi-Fi connectivity, app control, and automatic detergent dispensing for added convenience and efficiency in laundry tasks.</p></p>
