<p><strong>The Aerial Refueling Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Probe and Drogue</li><li>Boom and Receptacle</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aerial refueling systems are classified into two main types - Probe and Drogue, and Boom and Receptacle. Probe and Drogue systems involve a probe on the receiving aircraft that connects with a flexible hose, or drogue, trailed behind the tanker aircraft. In Boom and Receptacle systems, a rigid boom extends from the tanker aircraft to the receiving aircraft's receptacle. Each system has its own advantages and limitations, depending on factors such as aircraft design and operational requirements.</p></p>
