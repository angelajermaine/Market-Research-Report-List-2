<p><p>PVC Flooring Market consists of various types such as Homogenous, Heterogeneous, Vinyl Tiles (VT), and Luxury Vinyl Tiles (LVT). Homogenous PVC flooring is made of a single layer of PVC and is durable and easy to maintain. Heterogeneous PVC flooring consists of multiple layers and offers design flexibility. Vinyl Tiles (VT) are individual tiles with various patterns and designs. Luxury Vinyl Tiles (LVT) are high-end vinyl tiles that mimic the look of natural materials like wood or stone, providing a luxurious appearance.</p></p>
