<p><strong>The Medical Blood Filters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Whole Blood Transfusion</li><li>Platelet Transfusion</li><li>Red Cell Transfusion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Medical blood filters are used in various transfusion processes to ensure the safety and efficacy of blood products. Whole blood transfusion filters remove impurities and pathogens from donated blood before it is transfused into a recipient. Platelet transfusion filters help separate platelets from other blood components for specific medical conditions. Red cell transfusion filters are used to remove leukocytes and other contaminants from red blood cells to prevent adverse reactions in recipients. Each type of blood filter plays a critical role in maintaining the quality of blood products for transfusion.</p></p>
