<p><p>The Polycarbonate Panel market consists of three main types: Multi-Wall Panels, Corrugated Panels, and Solid Panels. Multi-Wall Panels are hollow structured panels with multiple layers, offering insulation and strength. Corrugated Panels have a wavy surface for added strength and flexibility, making them ideal for roofing and siding applications. Solid Panels are dense, single-layer panels known for their durability and impact resistance. Each type serves different purposes in construction and design, catering to a variety of needs in the market.</p></p>
