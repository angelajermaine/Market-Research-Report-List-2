<p><p>Integrated circuit leadframes are an essential component in electronic devices. The two main types of leadframes are stamping process leadframes and etching process leadframes. Stamping process leadframes involve pressing a metal sheet to create the desired shape, while etching process leadframes use a chemical process to remove material and create the leadframe design. Both types are widely used in the market, with each offering unique advantages in terms of cost, precision, and scalability for integrated circuit manufacturing.</p></p>
