<p><p>Methylamines market consists of three main types - Monomethylamine, Dimethylamine, and Trimethylamine. Monomethylamine is a primary amine with a wide range of industrial applications such as in the production of agricultural chemicals and pharmaceuticals. Dimethylamine is a secondary amine used in the manufacturing of herbicides and rubber chemicals. Trimethylamine is a tertiary amine commonly used as a precursor in the production of choline chloride and quaternary ammonium compounds. These methylamines play vital roles in various industries due to their versatile properties and applications.</p></p>
