<p><p>Bio-based engineering plastics are categorized into two main market types, injection and extrusion. Injection molding involves heating the plastic material and injecting it into a mold to create a desired shape, making it suitable for producing complex and detailed parts. On the other hand, extrusion involves melting the plastic material and forcing it through a die to create long, continuous shapes such as tubes, pipes, and sheets. Both methods play a crucial role in the production of bio-based engineering plastics for various industries.</p></p>
