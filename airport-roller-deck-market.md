<p><strong>The Airport Roller Deck Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Belt Connected</li><li>Chain Connected</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Airport roller decks come in two main types: belt connected and chain connected. Belt connected roller decks use a continuous looped belt to move luggage along the deck, providing a smooth and efficient transportation system. On the other hand, chain connected roller decks use interlocking chains to move luggage along the deck, ensuring sturdy and reliable transportation. Both types of roller decks play a crucial role in the efficient operation of airports, allowing for the seamless movement of passengers' luggage throughout the terminal.</p></p>
