<p><strong>The VR Lens Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-Fresnel Lens</li><li>Fresnel Lens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Non-Fresnel lenses in the VR market are traditional spherical lenses that provide high optical clarity and image quality. They are heavier and bulkier compared to Fresnel lenses but offer better overall performance. On the other hand, Fresnel lenses are lighter and thinner due to their unique design with concentric grooves that mimic the functions of traditional lenses. They are commonly used in VR headsets where weight and size are significant factors, offering a compromise between performance and portability.</p></p>
