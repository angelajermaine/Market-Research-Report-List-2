<p><strong>The Metal Forming Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bulk Forming</li><li>Sheet Forming</li><li>Powder Metal Forming</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metal forming equipment market includes three main types: bulk forming, sheet forming, and powder metal forming. Bulk forming involves shaping metal materials through processes such as forging, extrusion, and rolling. Sheet forming, on the other hand, involves bending and shaping metal sheets into desired shapes using equipment like press brakes and roll forming machines. Powder metal forming, on the other hand, involves compacting and sintering metal powders to create complex shapes and structures. Each type of metal forming equipment serves a unique purpose in the manufacturing industry.</p></p>
