<p><p>Air Ultrasonic Sensors Market consists of two main types: High Frequency Sensors and Low Frequency Sensors. High Frequency Sensors operate at frequencies above 20 kHz and are typically used for short-range applications that require high resolution and accuracy. On the other hand, Low Frequency Sensors operate at frequencies below 20 kHz and are suitable for applications that require longer range detection but with lower resolution. Both types of sensors have their own advantages and are used in various industries for different purposes.</p></p>
