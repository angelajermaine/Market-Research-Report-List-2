<p><p>Specialty packaging paper market types can be categorized into two main materials: wood pulp fiber and kraft paper. Wood pulp fiber material is derived from wood pulp, offering high strength and durability for packaging applications. On the other hand, kraft paper material is made from a chemical pulping process, providing excellent tear resistance and durability. Both materials are widely used in the packaging industry due to their sustainable and eco-friendly properties, catering to various packaging needs in different sectors.</p></p>
