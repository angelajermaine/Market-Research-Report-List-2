<p><p>Solar PV carports come in two main types: one-way carports and two-way carports. One-way carports have solar panels mounted on one side of the structure, providing shade for parked vehicles while generating electricity. Two-way carports have solar panels installed on both sides, doubling the energy production capacity. Both types offer the benefits of generating renewable energy, reducing electricity costs, and providing covered parking spaces. Consumers can choose the type of carport based on their energy needs and space availability.</p></p>
