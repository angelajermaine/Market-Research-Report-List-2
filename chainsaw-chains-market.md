<p><p>Chainsaw chains come in two main market types: full chisel and semi chisel. Full chisel chains are designed for fast and efficient cutting, making them ideal for professionals or those tackling tough materials. They have square-cornered teeth that are more aggressive and cut through wood quickly. On the other hand, semi chisel chains have rounded teeth that are more durable and stay sharp longer. These chains are better suited for occasional users or those working in dirty or abrasive conditions. Choose the type of chain best suited for your needs based on your cutting requirements.</p></p>
