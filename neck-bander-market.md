<p><p>The neck bander market is divided into two main types: heated and regular. Heated neck banders use heat to shrink the band around the neck of a product for a secure fit, ensuring tamper-evident packaging. On the other hand, regular neck banders do not use heat and rely on pressure to secure the band in place. Both types of neck banders are commonly used in industries such as food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, and cosmetics for packaging and labeling products.</p></p>
