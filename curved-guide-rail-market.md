<p><ul><li>Linear Part</li><li>Curve Part</li><li>360 Degree Loop Part</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Curved guide rail markets include three main types: linear part, curve part, and 360 degree loop part. The linear part is a straight section of the rail that provides stable guidance for objects moving in a specific direction. The curve part allows for smooth transitions around corners or bends in the track. The 360 degree loop part enables objects to travel in a continuous loop, providing endless possibilities for movement and design in various industries such as manufacturing and transportation.</p></p>
