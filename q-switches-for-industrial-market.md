<p><ul><li>Active Q Switch</li><li>Passive Q Switch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Q-switches are essential components in industrial laser systems that help control the output pulse width and peak power. The Active Q Switch market type involves using an external element to actively modulate the laser output, such as an acousto-optic or electro-optic modulator. On the other hand, the Passive Q Switch market type involves using materials with saturable absorption properties to passively control the laser output. Both types are crucial for applications in various industrial sectors, including materials processing, medical devices, and telecommunications.</p></p>
