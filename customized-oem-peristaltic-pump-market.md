<p><ul><li>Single Channel</li><li>Multi Channel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Customized (OEM) Peristaltic Pump market offers two main types - Single Channel and Multi Channel pumps. Single Channel pumps have one tubing channel, perfect for applications that require precise flow rates and dispensing. On the other hand, Multi Channel pumps have multiple tubing channels, allowing for simultaneous pumping of different fluids. This type is ideal for applications where multiple fluids need to be dispensed or mixed. Both types offer customization options to tailor the pump to specific user requirements.</p></p>
