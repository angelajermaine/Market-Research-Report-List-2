<p><p>Impulse Heat Sealers are categorized into two main types: Foot Impulse Heat Sealers and Hand Impulse Heat Sealers. Foot impulse sealers are designed to be operated using foot pedal controls, making them ideal for high-volume sealing tasks. On the other hand, hand impulse sealers are manually operated, making them more suitable for smaller-scale sealing jobs. Both types of sealers are commonly used in various industries for sealing plastic bags, pouches, and packaging materials to maintain product freshness and integrity.</p></p>
