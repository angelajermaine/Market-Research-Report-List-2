<p><p>Interface converters are designed to enable seamless communication between devices with different interface types. Singlemode interface converters are used for long-distance communication over fiber optic cables, providing high bandwidth and low signal loss. Multimode interface converters are ideal for shorter distances and offer cost-effective solutions for within-building communication. Both types of interface converters are essential for connecting various devices in networks, ensuring reliable data transmission across different interface types.</p></p>
