Semiconductor Piezoresistive Sensors utilize semiconductors to measure pressure changes. Elastic Strain Sensors detect pressure by measuring the deformation of a material. Thick Film Pressure Sensors use a thick film on a ceramic substrate to measure pressure. Ceramic Piezoresistive Sensors utilize ceramic materials to detect pressure changes. These different types of sensors cater to various applications in the oil pressure sensor market.</p></p>
