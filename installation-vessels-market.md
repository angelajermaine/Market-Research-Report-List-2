<p><ul><li>Shallow Sea</li><li>Deep Sea</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Installation vessels market can be categorized into two types based on the depth of the sea where the vessels operate - Shallow Sea and Deep Sea market. Shallow Sea installation vessels are designed to work in waters with relatively low depths, typically up to 200 feet. These vessels are suitable for installing structures near shorelines or in relatively shallow waters. Deep Sea installation vessels, on the other hand, are equipped to handle more challenging conditions in deeper waters, typically beyond 200 feet. These vessels are capable of installing structures in offshore locations farther from shore.</p></p>
