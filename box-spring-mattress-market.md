<p><strong>The Box Spring Mattress Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Linked Spring</li><li>One-Line Rigid Spring</li><li>Independent Canister Spring</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Box spring mattresses are categorized into different types based on the spring system used. Linked spring mattresses have a continuous row of coils connected to each other, providing firm support. One-line rigid spring mattresses contain coils aligned in a single row for consistent support. Independent canister spring mattresses have individual coils encased in fabric pockets, reducing motion transfer. Other types may include memory foam, latex, or hybrid mattresses. Each type offers unique benefits in terms of support, comfort, and durability.</p></p>
