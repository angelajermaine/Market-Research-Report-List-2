<p><strong>The Sports Drinks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Isotonic Sport Drink</li><li>Hypertonic Sport Drink</li><li>Hypotonic Sport Drink</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sports drinks market is categorized into three main types based on their concentration of solutes compared to body fluids. Isotonic sports drinks have a similar concentration to body fluids and are ideal for maintaining hydration during moderate-intensity exercise. Hypertonic sports drinks have a higher concentration of solutes and are used for rapid rehydration after intense physical activity. Hypotonic sports drinks have a lower concentration of solutes and are best for athletes looking to quickly replenish fluids without consuming excess calories or electrolytes.</p></p>
