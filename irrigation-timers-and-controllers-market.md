<p><p>Irrigation timers and controllers come in various types including smart controllers, tap timers, and basic controllers. Smart controllers utilize weather data and soil moisture levels to adjust watering schedules, maximizing water efficiency. Tap timers are simple devices that attach directly to a tap and allow for manual watering scheduling. Basic controllers offer standard programming options for setting watering times and durations. Each type of irrigation timer and controller caters to different levels of automation and complexity in managing watering systems.</p></p>
