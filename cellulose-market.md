<p><p>Cellulose market consists of two main types: natural cellulose and synthetic cellulose. Natural cellulose is derived from plants such as wood pulp, cotton, and hemp, and is used in products like paper, textiles, and food additives. Synthetic cellulose, on the other hand, is produced through chemical processes using materials like wood chips or cotton linters. It is commonly used in the manufacturing of films, membranes, and coatings. Both types of cellulose have unique properties that make them suitable for various industrial applications.</p></p>
