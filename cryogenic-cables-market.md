<p><p>Cryogenic cables are used in applications requiring extremely low temperatures, such as in medical and scientific research. The two main types of cryogenic cables are single-core and multi-core cables. Single-core cryogenic cables consist of a single conductor surrounded by insulation, while multi-core cables contain multiple conductors within a single cable. Both types are designed to withstand the challenges posed by low temperatures and ensure reliable transmission of power or data in cryogenic environments.</p></p>
