<p><strong>The Photoresist Cleaners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Photoresist (PR) Strippers</li><li>Negative Photoresist (PR) Strippers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Photoresist cleaners market consists of two main types - Positive Photoresist (PR) Strippers and Negative Photoresist (PR) Strippers. Positive PR strippers are used to remove photoresist material from areas where it is desired to remain on the substrate, while Negative PR strippers are used to remove photoresist material from areas where it is undesired to remain on the substrate. Both types of cleaners play a crucial role in the semiconductor industry for the production of electronic components.</p></p>
