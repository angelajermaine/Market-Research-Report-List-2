<p><p>The Mass Spectrometry Detector Market includes various types such as Gas Chromatography-Mass Spectrometry (GC-MS), Liquid Chromatography-Mass Spectrometry (LC-MS), and other types. GC-MS combines the chromatographic separation capabilities of gas chromatography with the mass analysis capabilities of mass spectrometry. LC-MS combines liquid chromatography separation with mass spectrometry detection. Other types of mass spectrometry detectors may include ICP-MS, MALDI-TOF, and SIMS. These detectors are used in various industries such as pharmaceuticals, environmental testing, and forensic analysis for precise and accurate measurements.</p></p>
