<p><p>Flash memory can be broadly categorized into two main types - NOR Flash and NAND Flash. NOR Flash is commonly used in applications where low latency and high read speeds are required, such as in firmware storage and code execution. On the other hand, NAND Flash is more commonly used in consumer electronic devices like smartphones and USB drives due to its higher storage capacity and cost-effectiveness. Both types of flash memory have their own market segments and cater to different use cases based on their unique characteristics and performance attributes.</p></p>
