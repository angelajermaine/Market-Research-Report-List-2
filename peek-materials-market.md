<p><p>PEEK materials are commonly categorized into two main market types: Pure PEEK Resin and Modified Resin. Pure PEEK Resin refers to the unaltered form of PEEK polymer, known for its high temperature resistance, chemical inertness, and excellent mechanical properties. In contrast, Modified Resin involves the incorporation of additives or fillers to enhance specific properties such as wear resistance, lubricity, or conductivity. Both market types cater to diverse industries such as aerospace, automotive, and medical, offering a wide range of application possibilities.</p></p>
