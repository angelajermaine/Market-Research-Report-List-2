<p><p>Automotive Weigh in Motion systems utilize various technologies for measuring the weight of vehicles while in motion. Strain gauges measure deformation in the structure caused by vehicle weight, fiber optical sensors detect weight based on light transmission changes, load cells measure weight through deformation of a material, and laser-based systems use lasers to calculate weight through displacement. Each type has its own advantages and applications, catering to different needs within the automotive industry.</p></p>
