<p><p>Scotch Whisky market is divided into three main types: Pure Malt Whisky, Grain Whisky, and Blended Whisky. Pure Malt Whisky is made from malted barley in pot stills, giving it a rich and complex flavor. Grain Whisky is made from grains other than malted barley and is often lighter in taste. Blended Whisky is a combination of Pure Malt and Grain Whiskies, carefully blended to achieve a balanced and smooth flavor profile. Each type caters to different consumer preferences and occasions within the Scotch Whisky market.</p></p>
