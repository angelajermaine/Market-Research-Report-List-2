<p><p>Polynorbornene is a type of polymer that can be produced through addition polymerization or open-loop metathesis polymerization (ROMP). Addition polymerization involves the joining of monomers without the production of any byproducts, resulting in a high purity polymer. ROMP, on the other hand, involves the breaking of carbon-carbon double bonds and their rearrangement to form the polymer. Both methods are utilized in different applications within the Polynorbornene market, catering to specific needs for purity and structure.</p></p>
