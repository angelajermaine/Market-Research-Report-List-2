<p><p>Solid color masterbatches are additives used in plastics manufacturing to give products a specific color. Two common types are standard size pellets and micro size pellets. Standard size pellets are larger, making them suitable for industries that require large volumes of color. Both types offer different advantages depending on the specific needs of the manufacturing process.</p></p>
