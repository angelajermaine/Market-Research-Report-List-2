<p><p>LiCoO2 batteries are primarily categorized into two market types - cylindrical and prismatic. Cylindrical batteries are compact and have a tubular shape, making them ideal for applications where space is limited, such as in laptops and power tools. Each market type offers unique advantages in terms of design flexibility and performance, catering to specific application requirements.</p></p>
