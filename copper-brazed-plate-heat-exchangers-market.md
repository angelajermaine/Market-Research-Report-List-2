<p><p>Copper brazed plate heat exchangers come in two main types: multi-circuit and single-circuit heat exchangers. Multi-circuit heat exchangers contain multiple fluid circuits within a single unit, allowing for higher efficiency and flexibility in heat transfer applications. On the other hand, single-circuit heat exchangers have only one fluid circuit, making them simpler and more cost-effective for basic heat transfer needs. Both types offer reliable and efficient heat exchange solutions for various industrial and commercial applications.</p></p>
