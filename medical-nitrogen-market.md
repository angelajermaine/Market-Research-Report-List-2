<p><ul><li>Gaseous State</li><li>Liquid State</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Medical Nitrogen Market can be divided into two main types based on its physical state: gaseous state and liquid state. Gaseous state nitrogen is stored and transported in pressurized cylinders and is commonly used in a variety of medical procedures such as cryosurgery. Liquid state nitrogen, on the other hand, is stored at very low temperatures and is often used in cryogenic applications like preserving biological samples. Both forms of medical nitrogen play crucial roles in the healthcare industry by providing a reliable source of inert gas for various medical purposes.</p></p>
