<p><p>Lamination adhesives for flexible packaging come in three main types: solvent-based adhesives, solvent-free adhesives, and water-based adhesives. Solvent-based adhesives use organic solvents as a carrier for the adhesive components. Solvent-free adhesives do not contain any solvents and are more environmentally friendly. Water-based adhesives use water as a carrier for the adhesive components. Each type of adhesive has its own benefits and drawbacks, making them suitable for different applications in the flexible packaging industry.</p></p>
