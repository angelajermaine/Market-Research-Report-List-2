<p><ul><li>Strain Type</li><li>Piezoresistive Type</li><li>Capacitive Type</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Low pressure sealed pressure sensors are classified into four main types: Strain Type, Piezoresistive Type, Capacitive Type, and Others. </p><p>Strain Type sensors measure pressure by detecting the strain in the sensor material. Piezoresistive Type sensors measure pressure by changes in electrical resistance. Capacitive Type sensors measure pressure by changes in the capacitor's capacitance. Other types include sensors that use different principles such as electromagnetic or optical to measure pressure. Each type has its own advantages and applications in various industries.</p></p>
