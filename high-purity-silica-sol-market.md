<p><p>High Purity Silica Sol is available in two main types: Hydrophilic Solvent and Hydrophobic Solvent market. Hydrophilic Solvent is a type of silica sol that has a strong affinity for water, making it ideal for applications where water is present. On the other hand, Hydrophobic Solvent is a type of silica sol that repels water, making it suitable for applications where a water-resistant coating is needed. Both types of silica sol offer unique properties and are used in various industries such as coatings, paints, and adhesives.</p></p>
