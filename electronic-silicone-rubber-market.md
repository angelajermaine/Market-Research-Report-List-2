<p><p>Electronic silicone rubber is categorized into two main types: transparent and opaque. Transparent silicone rubber is clear and allows light to pass through, making it ideal for applications where visibility is crucial, such as in LED displays or touchscreens. On the other hand, opaque silicone rubber blocks light and is commonly used in applications where insulation or protection is needed, such as in electronic enclosures or gaskets. Both types offer excellent flexibility, heat resistance, and durability, making them popular choices in the electronics industry.</p></p>
