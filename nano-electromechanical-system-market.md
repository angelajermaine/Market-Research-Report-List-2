<p><p>Nano Electromechanical System (NEMS) market includes various types such as nanotubes, nanowires, nanofilms, nanobelts, and others. Nanotubes are cylindrical tubes made of carbon atoms with high electrical and thermal conductivity. Nanowires are thin wires with diameters on the nanoscale, used for electronic and sensing applications. Nanofilms are thin layers of material, widely used in electronic devices. Nanobelts are nanostructures with high aspect ratios, providing unique mechanical and electrical properties. Other types of NEMS include nanoparticles, nanorods, and nanosprings.</p></p>
