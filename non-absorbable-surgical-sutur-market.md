<p><strong>The Non-absorbable Surgical Sutur Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Non-absorbable surgical sutures can be classified into two main types: Type I and Type II. Type I sutures are made from materials such as silk, nylon, or polyester, which are not absorbed by the body and thus need to be removed once the wound has healed. Type II sutures, on the other hand, are made from materials like stainless steel wire or polypropylene, which are used for internal suturing as they do not need to be removed and are generally left in place permanently.</p></p>
