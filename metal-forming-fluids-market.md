<p><strong>The Metal Forming Fluids Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Neat Oils</li><li>Emulsified (Soluble) Oils</li><li>Semi-Synthetics</li><li>Water Based Emulsifiable Synthetic Fluids</li><li>Water Based Synthetic Fluid Solutions</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metal forming fluids are categorized into different types based on their composition and intended use. Neat oils are pure oils used for lubrication during metal forming processes. Emulsified oils are mixtures of oil and water that provide both lubrication and cooling properties. Semi-synthetics contain a blend of mineral oil and synthetic additives. Water-based emulsifiable synthetic fluids are made from synthetic compounds that mix with water for cooling and lubrication. Water-based synthetic fluid solutions are fully synthetic fluids that provide superior performance. Other types of metal forming fluids include cutting fluids, grinding fluids, and quenching oils.</p></p>
