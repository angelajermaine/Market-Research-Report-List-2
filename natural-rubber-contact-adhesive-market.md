<p><ul><li>Solvent-Based Adhesive</li><li>Waterborne Adhesive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Natural Rubber Contact Adhesives can be classified into two main types: Solvent-Based Adhesives and Waterborne Adhesives. Solvent-Based Adhesives contain volatile organic compounds and require proper ventilation during application. They provide strong initial bond strength and are commonly used in industrial applications. On the other hand, Waterborne Adhesives are more environmentally friendly as they have lower VOC content. They offer good heat and chemical resistance, making them suitable for various applications in construction, automotive, and woodworking industries.</p></p>
