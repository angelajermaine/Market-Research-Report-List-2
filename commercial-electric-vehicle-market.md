<p><p>The Commercial Electric Vehicle market includes different types of vehicles powered by electricity: Battery Electric Vehicles (BEV), Hybrid Electric Vehicles (HEV), Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEV), and Fuel Cell Electric Vehicles (FCEV). BEVs run purely on electricity stored in a battery, while HEVs combine an internal combustion engine with an electric motor. PHEVs can be charged via an electrical outlet and have both electric and gasoline-powered engines. FCEVs use hydrogen fuel cells to generate electricity for propulsion. Each type offers unique benefits and capabilities for commercial use.</p></p>
