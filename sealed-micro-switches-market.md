<p><p>Sealed micro switches come in three main types based on their size and construction: sealed miniature, sealed subminiature, and sealed ultra subminiature. Sealed miniature switches are larger and offer more durability and reliability, making them suitable for various applications. Sealed subminiature switches are smaller in size and offer a more compact design for limited space applications. Sealed ultra subminiature switches are the smallest and most compact, ideal for applications where space is extremely limited while still maintaining high performance and reliability.</p></p>
