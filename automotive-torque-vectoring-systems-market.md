<p><strong>The Automotive Torque Vectoring Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Torque Vectoring System (ATVS)</li><li>Passive Torque Vectoring System (PTVS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive Torque Vectoring Systems utilize different technologies to improve vehicle performance and handling. Active Torque Vectoring Systems (ATVS) actively distribute torque between individual wheels to enhance traction and maneuverability. On the other hand, Passive Torque Vectoring Systems (PTVS) achieve similar outcomes through mechanical means such as limited-slip differentials. Both systems ultimately aim to optimize the distribution of torque to improve stability, cornering, and overall driving experience in various road conditions.</p></p>
