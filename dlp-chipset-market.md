<p><p>The DLP chipset market comprises various types of components, including Digital Micromirror Devices (DMD), DMD controllers, DMD micromirror drivers, and evaluation boards. DMDs are responsible for creating the image, while DMD controllers manage the operation of these devices. DMD micromirror drivers control the movement of the micromirrors within the DMD. Evaluation boards are used for testing and assessing the performance of DLP chipsets. Each component plays a crucial role in delivering high-quality display and imaging solutions in various applications.</p></p>
