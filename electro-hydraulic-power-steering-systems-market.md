<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>C-EHPS</li><li>P-EHPS</li><li>R-EHPS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>EHPS systems are divided into three main types:</p><p>1. C-EHPS (Column-Electro Hydraulic Power Steering System): This type combines an electric motor with a hydraulic pump to assist the steering column.</p><p>2. P-EHPS (Pinion-Electro Hydraulic Power Steering System): This type uses an electric motor directly connected to the steering pinion to provide steering assistance.</p><p>3. R-EHPS (Rack-Electro Hydraulic Power Steering System): This type has an electric motor connected to the steering rack, which helps in providing steering assistance.</p></p>
