<p><p>Low-Light-Level Detection Modules are specialized sensors used in various industries to detect and measure low levels of light. The Analog Module market consists of sensors that produce continuous output signals proportional to the amount of light detected, making them ideal for applications requiring precise measurements. On the other hand, the Digital Module market includes sensors that provide binary output signals indicating the presence or absence of light, making them suitable for simpler detection applications where accuracy is less critical. Both types of modules offer unique advantages depending on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
