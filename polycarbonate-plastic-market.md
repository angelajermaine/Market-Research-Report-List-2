<p><p>Polycarbonate plastic market is divided into different types based on specific characteristics and functionalities. Standard grade polycarbonate is used for general purpose applications, while high flow grade offers improved flow properties. High intensity grade provides enhanced impact resistance, optical grade offers superior clarity and light transmission. Flame retardant grade is used for applications requiring fire resistance. Other types include custom grades tailored to specific requirements such as UV resistance or chemical resistance. Each type caters to a different set of applications in various industries.</p></p>
