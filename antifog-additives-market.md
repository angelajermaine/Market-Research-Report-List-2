<p><p>The antifog additives market can be divided into four main types: glycerol esters, polyglycerol esters, sorbitan esters of fatty acids, and ethoxylated sorbitan esters. Glycerol esters are derived from glycerol and fatty acids, while polyglycerol esters are derived from polyglycerol and fatty acids. Sorbitan esters of fatty acids are produced by reacting sorbitan with fatty acids, and ethoxylated sorbitan esters are created through the ethoxylation of sorbitan esters. These additives are commonly used in various industries to prevent fogging on surfaces.</p></p>
