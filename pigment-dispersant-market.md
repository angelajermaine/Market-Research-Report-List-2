<p><p>Pigment dispersants are chemical additives used in various industries to disperse pigments evenly in a solution. There are four main types of pigment dispersants: nonionic, cationic, anionic, and amphoteric. Nonionic dispersants do not carry an electrical charge, cationic dispersants carry a positive charge, anionic dispersants carry a negative charge, and amphoteric dispersants carry both positive and negative charges. Each type of dispersant has specific applications based on the characteristics of the pigments and the desired outcome.</p></p>
