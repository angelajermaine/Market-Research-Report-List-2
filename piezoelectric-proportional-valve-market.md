<p><strong>The Piezoelectric Proportional Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Valves with Bender Element</li><li>Jet Valves with Multilayer Actuator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Piezoelectric proportional valves with bender element utilize a piezoelectric material that changes shape when exposed to an electric field, allowing for precise control of fluid flow. On the other hand, jet valves with multilayer actuator use multiple layers of piezoelectric material to generate high force and fast response rates for fluid flow control. Both types of valves offer superior performance and accuracy in various applications such as flow control in medical devices, inkjet printers, and industrial automation systems.</p></p>
