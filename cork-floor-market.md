<p><strong>The Cork Floor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pasting Cork Floor</li><li>Lock Cork Floor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cork floor market types include pasting cork floor and lock cork floor. Pasting cork floor is installed by gluing the cork tiles directly onto the subfloor, providing a more traditional and permanent option. On the other hand, lock cork floor features a unique interlocking system that allows for easy installation without the need for adhesives, making it a popular choice for DIY enthusiasts. Both types offer the benefits of cork flooring, such as comfort, durability, and sustainability, catering to different preferences and needs in the market.</p></p>
