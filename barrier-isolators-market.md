<p><ul><li>Closed Type</li><li>Open Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Barrier isolators are primarily categorized into two types: closed type and open type. Closed type isolators provide a completely sealed environment where operators can carry out tasks without any risk of contamination. Open type isolators, on the other hand, allow for more flexibility as they have openings for material transfer or equipment access. Closed type isolators are commonly used in pharmaceutical manufacturing for handling hazardous substances, while open type isolators are suitable for less critical applications such as equipment sterilization and decontamination.</p></p>
