<p><p>Automotive smart sensor market consists of several types, including camera sensors, ultrasonic radar sensors, millimeter wave radar sensors, and laser radar sensors. Camera sensors use visual input to detect objects and potential hazards. Ultrasonic radar sensors emit sound waves to measure distance and detect objects. Millimeter wave radar sensors use radio waves to detect objects and provide accurate distance measurements. Laser radar sensors use laser beams to detect objects and measure their distance. Each type of sensor serves a specific purpose in enhancing automotive safety and functionality.</p></p>
