<p><ul><li>Without Water Trapper</li><li>With One Water Trapper</li><li>With Two Water Trapper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Disposable ventilator circuits come in three main types based on the number of water trappers included in the design. Circuits without water trappers are basic and do not have a mechanism to trap excess moisture. Circuits with one water trapper have one moisture-removing component, while circuits with two water trappers have an additional layer of protection against water buildup. Each type caters to varying levels of moisture control based on the needs of the patient and healthcare provider.</p></p>
