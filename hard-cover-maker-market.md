<p><p>A hard cover maker is a machine used for creating hard covers for books, notebooks, and other bound products. There are two main market types for hard cover makers: manual and semi-automatic. In the manual market, the machine requires manual labor to operate fully, including feeding materials and turning the pages. In the semi-automatic market, the machine automates some processes, such as gluing and pressing, but still requires human intervention for other tasks. Both types cater to different production needs and levels of automation.</p></p>
