<p><ul><li>Reactive Silicone Fluid</li><li>Non-Reactive Silicone Fluid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Modified silicone market is categorized into two main types: Reactive Silicone Fluid and Non-Reactive Silicone Fluid. Reactive silicone fluids are designed to chemically react with other materials, forming strong bonds and creating durable coatings or sealants. On the other hand, non-reactive silicone fluids do not undergo chemical reactions and are used as lubricants, surfactants, or in personal care products. Both types play important roles in various industries such as construction, automotive, electronics, and cosmetics.</p></p>
