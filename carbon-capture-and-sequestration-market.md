<p><ul><li>Industrial Process</li><li>Oxy-Combustion</li><li>Pre-Combustion</li><li>Post-Combustion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Carbon capture and sequestration (CCS) technology includes various market types. Industrial process CCS focuses on capturing and storing CO2 emissions from industrial sources. Oxy-combustion CCS involves burning fuel in pure oxygen to produce a concentrated stream of CO2 for capture. Pre-combustion CCS captures CO2 before combustion by converting fuel into a mixture of hydrogen and CO2. Post-combustion CCS captures CO2 after fuel combustion. Each market type offers unique solutions for reducing greenhouse gas emissions in different industries.</p></p>
