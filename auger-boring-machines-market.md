<p><ul><li>Soft Ground ABMs</li><li>Hard Rock ABMs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Auger Boring Machines (ABMs) are divided into two main types based on the ground they are intended to work in - Soft Ground ABMs and Hard Rock ABMs. Soft Ground ABMs are designed to bore tunnels in loose soil, sediment, and other soft materials, making them suitable for projects in urban areas where minimal ground disturbance is desired. On the other hand, Hard Rock ABMs are specifically engineered to handle challenging conditions such as solid rock, providing efficient tunneling solutions for more rugged terrains.</p></p>
