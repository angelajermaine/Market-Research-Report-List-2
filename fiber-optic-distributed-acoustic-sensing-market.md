<p><p>Fiber Optic Distributed Acoustic Sensing (DAS) is a technology that uses fiber optic cables to detect sound and vibrations along its length. There are two main types of applications for DAS technology: Distributed Acoustic Sensing Interrogator (DASI) and Distributed Acoustic Sensing Processor (DASP) markets. DASI involves using a single laser interrogator to analyze data along the fiber optic cable, while DASP involves processing the data after it has been collected. Both markets cater to industries such as oil & gas, security, and infrastructure monitoring.</p></p>
