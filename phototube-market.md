<p><p>Phototubes are a type of light-detecting device used in various industries, available in different market types such as Gas, Vacuum, and Electron Multiplier. Gas phototubes operate using a gas-filled chamber to detect light, while vacuum phototubes use a vacuum-sealed tube for light detection. Electron Multiplier phototubes amplify the electron signal produced by incoming light for increased sensitivity. Each market type offers unique capabilities and applications based on the specific requirements of the end user.</p></p>
