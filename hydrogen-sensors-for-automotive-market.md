<p><p>Hydrogen sensors for automotive applications come in various types including pressure sensors, temperature sensors, hydrogen exhaust sensors, and mass air flow sensors. Pressure sensors detect changes in hydrogen pressure to ensure safe operations. Temperature sensors monitor temperature levels to prevent overheating. Hydrogen exhaust sensors detect hydrogen leaks for early detection of potential hazards. Mass air flow sensors measure the amount of air entering the engine to optimize fuel efficiency. These sensors play crucial roles in maintaining the safety and performance of hydrogen-powered vehicles.</p></p>
