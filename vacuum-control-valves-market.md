Manual vacuum control valves are operated manually by adjusting a knob or lever to control the flow rate of the vacuum. On the other hand, electronic vacuum control valves are automated and can be controlled remotely using electronic signals. Both types of valves play a crucial role in maintaining optimal vacuum levels in various industrial applications.</p></p>
