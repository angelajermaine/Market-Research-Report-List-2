<p><p>The Multifunctional Orthopedic Surgical Robot market can be classified into three types based on their operation modes: Semi-Active, Active, and Passive. Semi-Active robots require partial human input during the surgery, combining the skills of the surgeon with robotic assistance. Active robots are fully automated, performing surgical tasks independently. Passive robots provide stability and support during surgery but do not actively participate in the procedure. Each type offers unique benefits and capabilities, catering to different surgical needs and preferences within the orthopedic field.</p></p>
