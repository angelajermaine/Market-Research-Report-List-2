<p><p>A medical implant is a device made of materials such as metal, ceramic, plastic, or tissue that is placed inside the body to replace a missing biological structure or support a damaged biological structure. Medical implants are used in various medical procedures such as joint replacement, dental implants, cardiac implants, and others to improve the quality of life of patients.</p><p>The global medical implant market is experiencing significant growth and is expected to continue to expand at a CAGR of 12.4% during the forecast period. The market growth is being driven by factors such as the increasing prevalence of chronic diseases, rising geriatric population, technological advancements in implant materials and design, and growing demand for minimally invasive surgeries. Additionally, the rising healthcare expenditures, favorable reimbursement policies, and increasing awareness about the benefits of medical implants are also contributing to the growth of the market.</p><p>Some of the latest trends in the medical implant market include the development of smart implants with wireless connectivity, personalized implants using 3D printing technology, and bioresorbable implants that dissolve in the body over time. Furthermore, collaborations between medical device manufacturers and healthcare providers to develop innovative implant products are also propelling the market forward.</p></p>
